Yakitate Japan

Yakitate Japan is about a boy named Azuma Kazuma, a male sixteen-year-old youth striving to make "Ja-pan", a national bread for his country. On his journey he headed to Tokyo and became a baker under the famous bread-making chain, Pantasia. Along his journey he befriended and competed with multiple bakers, slowly achieving his dream.
